# Product Overview

## Product Name
HR Attendance Portal *(working title — pending user confirmation)*

## What It Is
A role-based web application for HR teams to centrally manage company SOPs and rules, track employee attendance via face photo upload, and monitor SLA compliance through a live dashboard — all in a clean, simple UI.

## Users & Roles

Two distinct roles with separate access and capabilities:

- **HR (Admin)** — the primary operator. Uploads and manages SOP documents and company rules, oversees the full employee attendance record, and uses the SLA compliance dashboard to see who met attendance requirements and who did not.
- **Employee** — the end user. Submits daily attendance by uploading a face photo, views their own attendance history, and accesses published SOPs and company rules.

## Core Features

### HR Role
- SOP / rules upload and management: upload documents (PDF or similar), organize by category, publish to employees
- Employee directory: view all registered employees and their attendance status
- SLA compliance dashboard: real-time view of valid vs. flagged attendance per employee, per period
- Attendance reports: filter by date, employee, or department

### Employee Role
- Face-based attendance check-in: upload a face photo to register attendance for the day
- Personal attendance history: view own attendance log and SLA status
- SOP access: read the latest published company rules and SOPs

## Attendance SLA Logic
Each employee is evaluated against an attendance SLA (minimum required attendance). The dashboard marks each record as **Valid** (met the SLA) or **Flagged** (absent or insufficient attendance). HR sees the aggregate view; employees see their own.

## Positioning
Simple, focused, and operational. Built for daily use — not a heavyweight HRMS. The design prioritises clarity: employees know exactly what to do, HR sees exactly what they need.

## Tone & Brand
Professional, clean, approachable. Functional UI with clear role-based navigation. Calm colour palette; no clutter.

## Scope (MVP)
- Web application (browser-based)
- Two roles: HR Admin and Employee
- SOP upload and management
- Face photo upload for attendance
- SLA attendance compliance dashboard
