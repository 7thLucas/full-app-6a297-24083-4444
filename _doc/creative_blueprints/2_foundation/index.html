Foundation Validated

Manual attendance tracking and inconsistent SOP enforcement

The Problem

HR teams spend hours managing attendance sheets, updating scattered policy documents, and manually verifying employee compliance. Employees lack visibility into their own attendance status and SLA compliance, leading to confusion and missed deadlines.

The Solution

Organizations struggle with manual attendance processes, scattered documentation of HR policies, and lack of real-time visibility into employee compliance with standard operating procedures and SLAs.

Target Audience

Mid-market organizations with 50-500 employees seeking to digitize HR operations

Personal Motivation

Eliminate manual attendance friction and create transparency in compliance tracking
