The Problem

HR teams are buried
in manual work.

Attendance tracked on paper or spreadsheets — error-prone and slow to reconcile

SOPs and rules scattered across emails, drives, and printed binders — no single source of truth

No real-time SLA compliance view — violations surface only after damage is done

Hours wasted per week on manual reconciliation

~4–8 hrs / week lost per HR staff

The Solution

One portal.
Two roles.
Full visibility.

Employees check in with a face photo — no cards, no sheets, no disputes

HR uploads SOPs once — employees always see the current version

Live SLA dashboard flags attendance compliance instantly — no chasing, no guesswork
