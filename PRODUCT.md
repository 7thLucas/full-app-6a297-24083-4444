# PresenceHQ — Product Overview

## Product Name
PresenceHQ

## What It Is
A role-based HR web application for managing company SOPs/rules and tracking employee attendance via face photo upload, with an SLA compliance dashboard — all in a clean, simple UI.

## Users & Roles

Two distinct roles:

- **HR (Admin)** — Uploads and manages SOP/rules documents, views the full employee attendance record, and monitors SLA compliance via a live dashboard.
- **Employee** — Submits daily attendance by uploading a face photo, views own attendance history and SLA status, and reads published SOPs.

## Core Features

### HR Role
- SOP / rules upload and management: upload documents (PDF), organize/publish to employees
- Employee directory: view all registered employees and their attendance status
- SLA compliance dashboard: valid vs. flagged attendance per employee, per period
- Attendance reports: filter by date, employee, or department

### Employee Role
- Face-based attendance check-in: upload a face photo to register attendance for the day (no ML/facial recognition needed — just photo upload that marks attendance)
- Personal attendance history: view own attendance log and SLA status
- SOP access: read published company rules and SOPs

## Attendance SLA Logic
Each employee is evaluated against an attendance SLA (minimum required attendance days). The dashboard marks each record as **Valid** (met the SLA) or **Flagged** (absent or insufficient). HR sees the aggregate view; employees see their own.

## Positioning
Simple, focused, operational. Built for daily use. Design prioritises clarity: employees know exactly what to do, HR sees exactly what they need.

## Tone & Brand
Professional, clean, approachable. Functional UI with clear role-based navigation. Calm colour palette; no clutter.

## Scope (MVP)
- Web application (browser-based)
- Two roles: HR Admin and Employee
- SOP upload and management
- Face photo upload for attendance (stores/displays the photo, marks attendance)
- SLA attendance compliance dashboard
